Ongoing & past researches

My current research is developing DRACO4, a novel next-gen humanoid.

The DRACO 3 was a succesful humanoid platform that was fully developed in house at the HCR lab at UT with design novelties in its lower body rolling contact joint. However, the application of rolling contacts complicated the controls and inaccessible electronics placements made maintenance and upgrdes nearly impossible. This motivated the lab to push for the next generation of DRACO humanoid, the DRACO 4.

The DRACO 4 will be developed as a robust and user friendly humanoid by being maintainable and upgradable with full documentation. It will have a novel, low inertia upper body limb design that will enable both high performance and safe human robot interaction.
